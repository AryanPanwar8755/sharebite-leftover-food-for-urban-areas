FAQ

Is donated food safe?

We accept food within a short time window, ensure sealed packing, and follow a hygiene checklist during pickup.

Do households need to reveal identity?

No. Household donors can stay anonymous while still tracking their impact.

Can my business get CSR reports?

Yes, we provide monthly digital receipts, photos, and impact summaries suitable for CSR documentation.

Which areas are supported?

Pilot: Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Rohtak. Expansion planned to additional Tier-2/3 cities.
